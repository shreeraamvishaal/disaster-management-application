AI-Powered Disaster Management Platform

Harnessing the power of AI to transform disaster response.

Scope

This project is all about creating an AI-powered disaster management platform that collects real-time information from social media, news, and official sources. It categorizes and presents the most relevant data to help agencies respond faster and more efficiently.

Vision

Our aim is to build a tool that will speed up disaster response times, improve decision-making with real-time data, and ultimately save lives by providing accurate, timely information to those in charge.

Goals

  • Real-time alerts and information.
  • AI-driven insights for prioritizing disaster-related updates.
  • A user-friendly dashboard for quick access to data.
  • Multilingual support for diverse regions.
  • Coordinate volunteers and agencies effectively.

Real-Time Issues Solved

This project tackles the information overload from social media and news during disasters, ensuring that rescue teams get the right data at the right time. It also helps in areas with limited resources by mapping out evacuation points and providing offline access to crucial information, especially in areas with poor connectivity.
