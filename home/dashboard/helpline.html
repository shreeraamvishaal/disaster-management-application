Disaster Management Helplines

Quick access to important resources and helplines for disaster management

Police

For reporting crimes, emergencies, and general police assistance.

Fire

For reporting fire emergencies and requesting fire department services.

Ambulance

For medical emergencies and ambulance services.

Disaster Management

For emergency assistance during natural disasters or major accidents.

Women Helpline

For reporting crimes against women and seeking assistance.

Child Helpline

For issues related to children in distress or in need of support.

National Emergency Number

A unified emergency number to contact police, fire, ambulance, and other emergency services.

Mental Health Helpline

For mental health crises, refer to local or state-specific helplines. Some states have dedicated numbers for mental health support.
