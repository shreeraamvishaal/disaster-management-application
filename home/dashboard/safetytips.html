Disaster Management Safety Tips

Prepare, Stay Safe, and Recover

Before a Disaster

Stay Informed

Monitor local news, radio, and apps for updates. Sign up for emergency alerts.

Prepare an Emergency Kit

Include water, food, a first-aid kit, flashlight, medicines, and important documents.

Develop an Emergency Plan

Plan escape routes, meeting points, and identify nearby shelters.

Know Your Evacuation Zones

Understand evacuation routes and practice them. Stay prepared.

During a Disaster

Follow Official Guidelines

Obey evacuation orders and use designated shelters.

Stay Indoors

Shelter in a safe room, away from windows. In an earthquake, take cover.

Use Communication Tools

Keep phones charged and use social media for non-urgent messages.

Avoid Hazardous Areas

Steer clear of flooded areas, downed power lines, and unsafe structures.

After a Disaster

Check for Injuries and Hazards

Check yourself and others for injuries, and watch for hazards like gas leaks.

Report to Authorities

Inform local authorities of any hazards or trapped individuals.

Help Others

Assist neighbors in need, but prioritize your safety first.

Sanitize and Stay Healthy

Clean wounds and ensure access to safe water. Avoid contact with stagnant water.
